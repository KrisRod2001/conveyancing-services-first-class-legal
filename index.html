Overview of Conveyancing

Conveyancing is the legal process of transferring property ownership. At First Class Legal, a specialized team manages every aspect, ensuring straightforward and efficient transactions for residential properties in Melbourne and across Victoria.

From preparing contracts to completing settlements, their services cover all necessary steps, including strata title and company title properties.

Key Tasks Handled

  • Preparation and review of Contracts of Sale
  • Conducting property searches
  • Calculating taxes and rates adjustments
  • Managing electronic or traditional settlements
  • Notifying authorities of ownership changes
